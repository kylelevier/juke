## Competing Platforms

### NCSA (ncsasports.org)
- **Model:** Athletes pay fixed-term contracts; coaches always free
- **Athlete pricing:** $1,386–$4,200+ (5 tiers: Free, Champion, Elite, MVP, MVP+); pricing never shown publicly — only revealed in a sales call
- **Paid adds:** Recruiting coach, professionally-produced highlight videos (2–5), 1-on-1 guidance, IMG Academy+ access
- **Coach access:** 100% free — full 600K+ athlete database, search, message, follow, notes, event tools
- **Sports:** 31+ (multi-sport)
- **Key weakness:** Fixed contracts with 3-day cancellation window; pricing hidden; recruiting coaches are employees, not active college coaches; generic across all sports

### Hudl (hudl.com)
- **Model:** Teams/schools pay for Hudl; athletes upgrade individually
- **Athlete free:** Full video profile, college search, coach contacts, opt-in recruiting database
- **Athlete paid:** ~$299/yr — enhanced analytics, boosted visibility in search results
- **Coach access:** Institutional subscription (pricing not public); sport-specific
- **Sports:** All sports; dominant in football
- **Key insight:** Most HS athletes are already on Hudl via their team. Free tier is genuinely strong.

### FieldLevel (fieldlevel.com)
- **Model:** Athletes pay subscription tiers; coaches free/low-cost
- **Athlete pricing:** Free → Silver ($278/yr) → Gold ($470/yr) → Platinum ($758/yr)
- **Key free→paid gate:** "Who viewed you" + direct coach messaging unlocks at Gold
- **Coach access:** Free base; $99/yr Prime adds staff dashboard + bulk athlete Premium seats
- **Sports:** 30+
- **Unique feature:** Coaches post roster needs by position/grad year; athletes get matched alerts

### SportsRecruits (sportsrecruits.com)
- **Model:** Athletes pay; coaches always 100% free (no paid tier for coaches)
- **Athlete pricing:** Free → Pro (~$399/yr or ~$250/yr via club)
- **Key free→paid gate:** Coach view tracking, direct messaging, roster needs filters
- **Coach access:** Always completely free — full DB, messaging, export to CRM, EventBeacon
- **Sports:** 30+; official platform for IWLCA, IMLCA, NFHCA
- **Key insight:** Strongest in soccer, lacrosse, field hockey, volleyball; coach-friendliest model

### Perfect Game (perfectgame.org) — Baseball & Softball only
- **Model:** Event-based, not subscription; pay per tournament/showcase
- **Athlete cost:** $100–$600+ per event; free profile creation
- **Value prop:** Live exposure to 300+ NCAA D1 programs at events; scouting reports built through participation
- **Coach access:** Free event registration; free database access via NCSA integration
- **Key insight:** The sport-specific gold standard. College coaches in baseball/softball MUST attend PG events. This is the model Juke should study for volleyball.

### 247Sports / On3/Rivals
- **Model:** Media and rankings platform; NOT a self-service recruiting tool
- **Athletes:** Cannot create profiles or contact coaches — rankings are assigned by analysts
- **Paid:** $10–25/mo per school for insider recruiting news, message boards
- **Key insight:** Irrelevant to Juke's direct competitive set; only matters for elite football/basketball

## Volleyball-Specific Platforms

Volleyball has the richest sport-specific ecosystem of any major HS sport — directly comparable to what Perfect Game is for baseball.

### PrepDig (prepdig.com)
- **Part of:** Prep Network (also runs Prep Hoops, Prep Soccer, Prep Lacrosse, etc.)
- **Role:** Dominant media + scouting + prospect database + showcases for volleyball
- **Athlete free:** Player profile in prospect database, visible to coaches
- **Standard (paid):** Full article/rankings access (400+ articles/mo), profile view trends, recruiting task tracking
- **Prep Dig+ (paid):** Coach view notifications, College Match tool, coach contact info for every program, personalized email templates, $25 off showcases
- **Coach access:** Paid subscription for full prospect database access; free showcase attendance
- **Key insight:** NCSA is a PrepDig partner — PrepDig athletes can access NCSA tools. PrepDig has the largest volleyball scouting editorial operation in the country.

### University Athlete (universityathlete.com) ⭐ PRIMARY VOLLEYBALL INCUMBENT
- **Founded:** 2003 — 20+ years exclusively in volleyball; the longest-tenured sport-specific recruiting platform of any sport researched
- **Model:** Athletes pay subscription; coaches always free
- **Athlete free (Starter):** Profile creation, college browse, 5 video links, 20 AI-matched college suggestions, share interest with 20 programs, view college follows by division only (totals, not specific programs)
- **Athlete paid (All-Access):** $249/yr (regular $299/yr) — see exactly which colleges followed/viewed you, unlimited messaging, unlimited video uploads, BallerTV highlight clip creation, full AI-matched college list, event alerts (notified when a favorited college attends your tournament), search school needs
- **Key free→paid gate:** Same as every competitor — "who viewed you" is locked behind All-Access
- **Coach access:** Always 100% free — "the only volleyball recruiting platform that every D1 & D2 program uses to recruit potential athletes"; 5,000+ NCAA D1/D2/D3/JUCO/NAIA coaches
- **Coach endorsements:** Kevin Hambly (Stanford), Jill Wilson (LSU), Craig Dyer (Notre Dame), Erik Sullivan (US National Team), Kenzie Shand (Michigan), Morgan Thomas (TCU), Yale, Florida State, Nebraska, Penn State, Texas, USC, Arizona State
- **Part of:** Otto Sport AI ecosystem; related product is **SportWrench** (event management software) — creates a flywheel identical to Perfect Game: manage the tournaments → coaches use UA at those tournaments → athletes must be on UA to be seen
- **Integrations:** BallerTV (game film → highlight clips)
- **Key insight:** This is the dominant incumbent. The SportWrench + University Athlete flywheel is the volleyball equivalent of Perfect Game. Kevin Hambly's endorsement alone signals that the elite end of the coaching community considers this the standard tool.

### Set Point (setpointvolleyball.com)
- **Model:** Athletes pay low-cost subscription; coaches always 100% free permanently
- **Athlete free:** Full profile, College Readiness Score, NCAA Eligibility Tracker, Career Explorer, Financial Aid Estimator, college browse, 3 match previews, family dashboard
- **Athlete Pro:** $12.99/mo or $99/yr — 35 college matches with fit scores, full roster intelligence (1,100+ programs), Email Coach (20 AI drafts/mo), coach contacts
- **Athlete Elite:** $17.99/mo or $149/yr — unlimited everything, 10 references, development plans
- **Coach access:** Always 100% free — full search, watchlist, staff notes, NCAA-compliant messaging
- **Differentiators:**
  1. Real-time NCAA roster gap intelligence — scans every D1/D2/D3 roster daily for graduation gaps at athlete's position
  2. College readiness suite beyond recruiting (eligibility, career, financial aid)
  3. Priced at $99/yr vs. $1,500+ at NCSA — actively markets this gap
  4. Never sells athlete data; no third-party enrichment
- **Founded by:** Former D1 swimmer and his current college recruit daughter
- **Integrations:** Hudl (stats import)

## Adjacent / Pre-Recruiting Platforms to Watch

### Zorts / ZortsID (zortssports.com)
- **What it is:** Event management + athlete identity verification platform (tournament scheduling, scoring, payments, rostering)
- **ZortsID:** Verified athlete identity card — biometrics (Flash Biometrics partnership), age verification, headshot, anti-cheating enforcement; available via API for any platform to integrate
- **Video:** SportsEngine Play integration for game recordings and highlight clip creation
- **Events:** Hosts "National Recruiting Tournaments" — so college coaches are already in their ecosystem
- **What it hasn't built:** No college coach database, no athlete-to-coach connection tools, no profile analytics, no recruiting fit matching
- **Assessment:** Has the event infrastructure + verified athlete identity layer that logically underpins a recruiting platform — but the recruiting layer doesn't exist yet. The ZortsID API in particular is notable: verified identity with biometrics and photo is the foundation a recruiting profile needs, and they've already commoditized it. Watch this space.

## Universal Patterns Across All Platforms

### Coaches are always free
Every platform researched funds coach access through athlete subscriptions. This is not optional — it is the market standard. Juke must follow this model.

### The free→paid conversion gate is "who's watching you"
The single most consistent paid feature across every platform: **coach view notifications**. Athletes can be seen for free; knowing they're being seen costs money.

| Platform | Free | Paid unlocks |
|----------|------|-------------|
| FieldLevel | Profile visible | Who viewed you + messaging |
| SportsRecruits | Profile + video | Real-time coach view alerts + messaging |
| PrepDig | In database | Coach view notifications + coach contacts |
| Set Point | 3 match previews | Full roster intelligence + coach contacts |
| University Athlete | Profile + 20 matches + totals only | Which specific colleges viewed/followed you + messaging |

### Pricing philosophy: two camps
1. **Concierge model (NCSA):** $1,500–$4,200+, fixed contracts, human guidance. Targets anxious families.
2. **SaaS model (FieldLevel, SportsRecruits, Set Point):** $99–$758/yr, self-service, transparent pricing. Set Point explicitly markets "$99/yr vs. $1,500+ at NCSA."

## Juke Free vs. Paid Recommendation

### Non-negotiable: Coaches always free

### Athlete Free (always)
- Complete profile (stats, academic info, bio, video)
- Visibility in Juke's coach-searchable database
- College browse and search
- NCAA recruiting calendar / timeline education
- Basic recruiting process content

### Tier 1 — "I want to know what's happening" (FOMO tier)
*Primary conversion trigger — gate this after user base is established*
- Who viewed your profile, and when
- Alerts when a coach views your video or transcript
- Profile view count vs. peers in your class/sport
- Direct messaging to coaches

### Tier 2 — "I want to recruit proactively" (action tier)
- Roster intelligence — programs with graduation gaps at your position
- Program fit matching with scores
- Coach email templates personalized to each school
- Roster needs alerts

### Tier 3 — "I want the full picture" (readiness tier)
- NCAA eligibility tracker
- Financial aid / scholarship estimator
- College readiness score
- Parent/family dashboard

### Progressive Timeline
- **Launch (0–12 mo):** Keep Tier 1 free to drive adoption and build coach network
- **Growth (12–24 mo):** Gate "who viewed you" — this is the highest-converting moment in recruiting SaaS
- **Maturity (24+ mo):** Build out Tier 2 roster intelligence and Tier 3 readiness tools

### Pricing anchor
Set Point's framing is the sharpest in the market: *$99/year vs. $1,500+ at NCSA*. If Juke is sport-specific, this positioning is available and credible.
