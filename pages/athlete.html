💡 What college coaches want to see in women's flag football film ▸
🎯
Route running — Full routes from snap to separation. Coaches watch your feet, not just your hands.
🔄
Variety — Different plays and matchups. Don't show the same route five times.
🏆
Competitive moments — Championship games and close matchups beat blowouts every time.
⚡
Speed in context — Beating a defender matters more than running in open space.
🎬
Length — 2–3 minutes is ideal for a highlight reel. Coaches lose focus after 4 minutes.
📅
Keep it current — Update every season. Film from 2+ years ago gets discounted fast.
